Website Planning

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the CandyShop website is to sell candy to both customers and other small businesses around the area. Customers will be able to log in to the website and search for their favorite candy to purchase before placing an order. All local customers will have the courtesy to have their orders delivered to their desired location within 24 hours. Customers will be able to contact us should they need any more information since contact details will be provided. Custormer will be able to acces the website from the official domain "www.thecandystore.co.sz".

Service

The service that will be provided by the CandyShop website will sell strictly all kinds of well-known candies such as Reese's peanut butter cups, Twix, Snickers, M&M's, Gummi bears, Kit Kat, and many other new candies on the market will also be served at the CandyShop. The website will also be collecting data on the candy that is mostly purchased by customers. This will help in knowing which type of candy the shop should invest in more.

Branding: The Website official Logo

Audience

My key audience could be anybody from young kids who love candy to the older generation that loves candy as well. Working class people of all ages, whether male or female, are welcome at the CandyShop as long as you are a candy fan.
